My Favorite Movies

Kung Fu Panda

Synopsis: Po works at his family's noodle shop and dreams of becoming a kung fu master. His dream comes true when he is unexpectedly chosen to fulfill an ancient prophecy and must train in martial arts with his idols, the Furious Five.

Kung Fu Panda 2

Synopsis: Now living his dream as the Dragon Warrior, Po protects the Valley of Peace alongside his friends, the Furious Five. He faces a new villain who plans to use an unstoppable secret weapon to conquer China and destroy kung fu.

Kung Fu Panda 3

Synopsis: Po realizes he still has a lot to learn. After reuniting with his father, Po must move from student to master and train a group of pandas to defeat the evil Kai, a supernatural warrior who grows stronger with each battle.
